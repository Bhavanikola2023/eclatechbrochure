Frequently Asked Questions

What types of organizations do you work with?

We work with a diverse range of organizations including higher education institutions, K-12 schools, businesses, non-profits, government agencies, and community groups. Our approach is customized to the specific needs and context of each organization.

Do you offer virtual options for your services?

Yes, all of our services are available in virtual, in-person, or hybrid formats. Our virtual workshops and trainings maintain the same interactive, hands-on approach that makes our in-person sessions so effective.

How are your workshops and trainings structured?

Our programs follow a proven methodology that includes: 1) Introduction to AI fundamentals, 2) Live demonstrations of relevant applications, 3) Hands-on activities and practice, 4) Ethical considerations, and 5) Guided exploration and Q&A. This structure ensures participants gain both theoretical understanding and practical skills.

What is your typical timeline from enquiry to delivery?

After your initial enquiry, we typically schedule a discovery consultation within 1-2 business days. Following this conversation, we'll provide a customized proposal within 3-5 business days. The timeline from proposal acceptance to delivery varies based on the scope and complexity of your needs, but we can typically accommodate events with 2-8 weeks of lead time.

Do you provide materials and resources to participants?

Yes, all participants receive comprehensive resources including session recordings, customized toolkits, reference guides, and access to our resource portal for continued learning. These materials are designed to support ongoing implementation of the skills and knowledge gained during our programs.
